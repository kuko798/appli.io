Solution

One extension. Every application.
Zero manual work.

1

Connect Gmail

OAuth2 read-only access. Pull up to a year of application emails in one click.

2

A local LLM reads each email and assigns Applied, Interview, Offer, or Rejected — with a reason.

3

Dashboard fills in

Company, role, status, and date populate automatically. Filterable, sortable, always current.

4

Go deeper

Resume diagnostic, interview simulator, company deep scan, and AI follow-up generator — all built in.
